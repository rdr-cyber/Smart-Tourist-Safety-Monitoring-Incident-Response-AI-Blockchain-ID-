Smart Tourist Safety Monitoring & Incident Response System

An AI and Blockchain-powered solution for enhancing tourist safety through real-time monitoring, incident reporting, and secure identity verification

Real-time Monitoring

Continuous tracking of tourist locations with AI-powered risk assessment and predictive analytics for proactive safety measures.

Incident Response

Instant reporting and coordination system that connects tourists with emergency services and nearby response units.

Blockchain Identity

Secure digital identity verification using Decentralized Identifiers (DID) and Verifiable Credentials (VC) for trusted authentication.

Mobile & Web Interface

User-friendly applications for both tourists and safety personnel with intuitive dashboards and real-time updates.

Advanced Features

Itinerary risk scanner, digital safe, anonymous reporting, AI safety scores, and fraud detection for guides/taxis.

Analytics & Reports

Comprehensive data insights with predictive alerts, safety trends, and performance metrics for continuous improvement.
